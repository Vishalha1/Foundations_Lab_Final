Question 1: What does gpupdate /force do?
Answer: It forces Windows to refresh and apply the latest Group Policy settings immediately.

Question 2: What is LSDOU?
Answer: LSDOU stands for Local, Site, Domain, and Organizational Unit. It is the order Group Policy is applied.

Question 3: Why was the GPO linked to the Engineering OU?
Answer: It was linked to the Engineering OU so only the Engineering users would get the Control Panel restriction.
